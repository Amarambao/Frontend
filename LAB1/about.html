Welcome to Pao Family Tea House, the place where you can enjoy a cup of tea and a moment of relaxation.
We are a family-owned and operated tea shop, established in 2023, with a passion for quality and variety.
We offer a wide range of teas from around the world, from classic black and green teas to herbal and fruit infusions.
Whether you prefer a strong brew or a delicate aroma, we have something for you.

At Pao Family Tea House, we believe that tea is more than just a drink. It is a culture, a tradition, and a way of life.
That's why we source our teas from reputable and ethical suppliers, who respect the environment and the people who grow and harvest the tea leaves.
We also care about our customers and their well-being, which is why we use only natural ingredients and avoid artificial flavors and preservatives.

Our tea shop is not only a place to buy tea, but also a place to experience tea.
We invite you to join us for our daily tea tastings, where you can sample different teas and learn more about their history and benefits.
You can also browse our selection of tea accessories, such as pots, cups, strainers, and infusers, to make your tea time more enjoyable.
And if you need any advice or recommendations, our friendly and knowledgeable staff will be happy to assist you.

We hope to see you soon at Pao Family Tea House, where every cup of tea is a celebration of life.
